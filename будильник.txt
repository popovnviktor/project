Alarm clock designed to control time by means of sound notification tied to the time set by the user.
It uses a battery as a power supply.
Has two hands and a dial, a bell.



Positive testing:
1) Checking the accuracy of the display time;
2) Check for sound signal activation;

Negative testing:
1) Checking the performance of the lying position;
2) Test for falling from a height;
3) Checking for ease of use (how easy it is to understand the functionality);
4) Check for performance from a weak power supply.
